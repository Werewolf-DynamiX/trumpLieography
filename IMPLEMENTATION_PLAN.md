- Academic citations or "according to studies"

**Title:** The Definitive Fictional Lieography of Donald J. Trump: A Satirical Biography Written in Trump's Own Voice

**Description:**

This is not your typical biography.

*The Definitive Fictional Lieography of Donald J. Trump* is a boldly satirical work—a self-mythologized first-person account written entirely in Trump's unmistakable voice. It is a chronicle of legend-building, where reality is merely a starting point and the feeling of greatness matters infinitely more than factual accuracy.

From his birth in Queens (where, we're told, "the birds stopped singing") through his conquest of Manhattan, his reign as television's Oracle, his presidency marked by impossible accomplishments, and his promised restoration in 2025, this book presents a narrative where Trump tells his own story exactly as he'd like it remembered.

**What You'll Find Inside:**

- A preface explaining why Trump had to write his own biography ("Nobody else could do it right")
- A reluctant foreword by Michelle Obama, describing her relentless pursuit by Trump until she agreed to write it
- Chapters covering: The birth of greatness in Queens, the Manhattan empire, the Reality TV era, the political rise, the indictments and trials, and the promised restoration
- A publisher's afterword from a senior editor hiding in a supply closet, unable to fact-check the manuscript
- An ending that merges the man with the myth so completely that they become indistinguishable

**The Satirical Magic:**

Written with consistent Trump voice throughout—complete with superlatives ("tremendous," "incredible," "disaster"), the "people are saying" technique, third-person references to Trump's own mythology, and a worldview where winning and losing are the only categories that matter.

This is satire that works on multiple levels:
- For Trump supporters: A loving, mythologized tribute
- For Trump critics: A devastating portrait of ego and self-invention
- For satire enthusiasts: A master class in how personal branding overwrites reality

**Key Scenes Include:**
- A sandbox real estate deal at age 5
- The "Incident" at military academy
- Negotiating with Hillary over 3 AM calls
- The bailiff weeping while taking his mugshot
- Trump buying Greenland with an executive order
- Trump demanding the Moon be full every night (and getting NASA to work on it)

**Perfect For:**

- Political satire lovers who appreciate *The Onion* and dark political humor
- Trump supporters who enjoy seeing their hero celebrated unapologetically
- Trump critics who appreciate lampooning through exaggeration
- Readers interested in mythology, media, and the construction of public personas
- Anyone who found themselves asking: "What would Trump write about Trump?"

**About the Manuscript:**

When this manuscript arrived at the publisher's offices in a gold-embossed envelope delivered by helicopter, the legal team faced a choice: publish as satire or don't publish at all. They chose satire. Not because they fact-checked it—they gave up after one week—but because it's the only honest classification for a book that admits, in its title, that it is fictional.

No claims have been verified. No statistics have been checked. The author insisted that facts are "suggestions" and reality is negotiable. The publisher's legal department had a complete breakdown and transferred to Zurich.

**The Bottom Line:**

This is a book that will make you laugh, uncomfortable, angry, or validated—depending on your perspective. And that's exactly the point.

*Read it. Learn from it. Decide for yourself who the real Trump is.*
