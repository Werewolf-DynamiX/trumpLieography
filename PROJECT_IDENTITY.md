# PROJECT IDENTITY: The Definitive Fictional Lieography of Donald J. Trump

## 1. Vision & Thesis
**Thesis Statement:** This satirical "Lieography" exposes the absurdity of truth-as-branding by embodying Trump's self-mythologizing voice so perfectly that readers cannot distinguish parody from reality. The humor emerges from the gap between the narrator's grandiose claims and observable reality.

**Target Audience:** Readers of political satire (age 25-65), political junkies who appreciate meta-commentary, and collectors of Trump-related cultural artifacts.

## 2. Strategic Pillars
1. **Voice Authenticity:** The "Trump Voice" must be indistinguishable from the real thing at a sentence level. Uncanny valley satire.
2. **Satirical Distance:** Absurdity escalates to signal fiction (crying bailiffs, Moon feuds).
3. **Structural Comedy:** "Straight man" elements (Forewords, Publisher's Notes) provide relief and grounding.
4. **KDP Viability:** Lean word count, scannable formatting, clear fiction signaling for legal protection.

## 3. Style & Voice Overrides
**Register:** Register D — The Unreliable Narrator (The "Trump Voice").
**Tone:** "Truthful Hyperbole." High velocity, staccato, confident, rambling but rhythmic.

### Diction & Syntax
- **Sentence Structure:** Fragmented. Repetitive. Punchy. "Many people are saying." "It's true."
- **Vocabulary:** Superlatives (greatest, huge, disaster, sad, nasty).
- **Rhythm:** Short sentence -> Short sentence -> Very long, run-on sentence with multiple tangents -> Short summary.

## 4. Operational Overrides
- **De-AI-ification:** Ruthlessly scrub all academic or balanced language. No "nuance," no "on the other hand."
- **Legal Safety:** Ensure fiction signaling is clear in front matter and back matter.
- **Fact-Checking:** Verify *real* events to pivot from them effectively.

## 5. Technical Reference
- **Format:** Satirical Biography / Political Humor
- **Word Count Target:** 10,000-15,000 words
- **Status:** Draft complete (~14,000 words).
