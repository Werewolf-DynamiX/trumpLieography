## Voice Consistency Checklist

Before finalizing each chapter, verify:

- [ ] Minimum 3 superlatives per page
- [ ] At least one "people are saying" or "everyone knows" per section
- [ ] Brand Capitalization applied (The Deal, The Tower, etc.)
- [ ] Short, punchy sentences dominate
- [ ] Third-person "Trump" references for mythic moments
- [ ] Rivals have nicknames
- [ ] All failures reframed as victories or persecution
- [ ] The word "tremendous" appears at least once per 500 words

---

## Sensitive Content Guidelines

Handle these topics with satirical distance while maintaining the voice:

1. **Race-related controversies** - Satirize the denial patterns
2. **Sexual misconduct allegations** - Focus on the "witch hunt" framing he uses
3. **January 6** - Satirize the mythologizing, not the violence
4. **COVID deaths** - Satirize the self-congratulation
5. **Family dynamics** - Satirize the dynasty obsession

The satire should expose the absurdity of the self-mythology without becoming mean-spirited toward victims.
