## 5. COVER DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Visual Brief for Designer

**Tone:** Ostentatious, satirical, unmistakably Trump

**Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** Gold (#FFD700 or Trump gold)
- **Secondary:** Deep red (#C41E3A) or black
- **Accent:** White for text contrast

**Required Elements:**
1. **Title:** Bold, gold lettering - "The Definitive Fictional Lieography of Donald J. Trump"
2. **Subtitle:** "A Satirical Biography" - must be visible
3. **Author:** "Donald J. Trump" - prominent placement
4. **Satire Signal:** Visual cue that this is humor (see options below)

**Visual Style Options:**
- Option A: Gold silhouette on black background
- Option B: Luxury branding aesthetic (art deco, gold leaf)
- Option C: Text-as-design with repeating "TRUMP"
- Option D: Minimalist gold geometric

**Technical Requirements:**
| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Trim Size** | 6" x 9" |
| **Bleed** | 0.125" all sides |
| **Final File Size** | 6.25" x 9.25" |
| **Resolution** | 300 DPI minimum |
| **Color Mode** | CMYK (for print) |
| **File Format** | PDF (press-ready) |

**What to AVOID:**
- Real photographs of Trump (copyright)
- Trademarked Trump Organization logos
- Cartoonish caricatures (legal gray area)
- Anything that looks like official Trump endorsement
- Political symbols that overshadow satire signal

**Budget Range:** $200-500 depending on designer

**Designer Sources:**
- Fiverr: Search "political satire book cover"
- 99designs: Crowdsource multiple options
- Reedsy: Professional book designers

## 6. LEGAL DISCLAIMERS

### Required Copyright Page Text
```
Copyright 2026

This is a work of satirical fiction. All statements, assertions,
and claims are presented within the context of a fictional narrative.
This book does not represent factual biography or historical record.

The author has taken extensive creative liberties with facts, timelines,
events, and dialogue. Any resemblance between fictional events and
actual events is entirely intentional but legally deniable.

This work is protected as satire and parody under fair use doctrine.

All rights reserved. No part of this publication may be reproduced
without written permission from the publisher.
```

### Additional Legal Protection
- Title includes "Fictional" - explicit fiction signal
- Publisher's Note frames as satirical (already in manuscript)
- Michelle Obama foreword signals parody
- No defamatory claims beyond public record
- No incitement language

### Legal Risk Assessment
| Risk | Level | Mitigation |
|------|-------|------------|
| Defamation | LOW | All claims are documented Trump rhetoric |
| Copyright | LOW | No protected material used |
| Trademark | LOW | No Trump Org logos/imagery |
| Parody Defense | STRONG | Clear satire throughout |
