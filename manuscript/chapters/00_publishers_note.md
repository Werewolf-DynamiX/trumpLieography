## **A Note on This Edition**

*The following manuscript arrived at our offices in a gold-embossed envelope delivered by helicopter. It was accompanied by a handwritten note reading: "Print this exactly as written. Do not fact-check. The facts would only slow it down."*

*After extensive legal review, we have determined that this work is best classified as "satirical fiction"—a genre that allows us to publish it without liability while also allowing readers to interpret it however they wish.*

*Some readers will see a loving tribute. Others will see a devastating critique. Still others will see a mirror held up to an era when truth became negotiable and confidence became indistinguishable from competence.*

*We see all of these things. We also see our legal team's invoices.*

*This book is a work of fiction. Any resemblance to actual events, actual hubris, or actual gold-plated bathroom fixtures is entirely intentional but legally deniable.*

*Read at your own risk. Whose risk? Your risk.*

*—The Publishers*
