## **Chapter 2: The Manhattan Conquest**
### *1970s–1989*

---

### **Crossing the River**

1971. I crossed the bridge. Queens to Manhattan.

Manhattan was a disaster. Crime. Graffiti. Broke.

Fred said, "Don't go."

I said, "I'm going."

I looked at the skyline. I didn't see buildings. I saw my name. In gold.

---

### **Studio 54**

The club. The only club.

If you weren't there, you were dead.

I walked in. The velvet rope parted. Moses and the Red Sea.

I saw everyone. Andy Warhol. He sketched me. "Donald," he said. "You are the future."

I learned the lesson: Be seen. Be loud. Be everywhere.

---

### **The Tower**

Fifth Avenue. Tiffany's. The best corner in the world.

I built a palace.

Bronze glass. Pink marble. A waterfall.

The critics hated it. "Too flashy," they said.

The people loved it. They lined up. They bought apartments.

And the name. T-R-U-M-P.

Before me, buildings were just numbers. "725 Fifth Avenue." Boring.

I gave them a name. I gave them a soul.

---

### **The Hits**

I was on a roll. Unstoppable.

**The Commodore.** A dump next to Grand Central. I covered it in glass. Made it a jewel. Zero money down. Magic.

**The Rink.** Wollman Rink. The city couldn't fix it. Six years! I fixed it in four months. Under budget. The ice was perfect.
> *[Publisher’s Note: City records dispute this timeline, but we have been advised by our Legal team not to pursue it further.]*

**The Plaza.** The Mona Lisa. I bought it. I owned the lobby from *Home Alone 2*.

**The Yacht.** *Trump Princess*. Huge. A disco. A helipad. I don't even like boats. But I liked having the biggest one.

I was the King of New York.

---

### **The Art of the Deal**

1987. I wrote the book.

Number one bestseller. Forever.

Simple rules:
1.  Think Big.
2.  Fight Back.
3.  Never Quit.

It’s the Bible of business. Actually, it’s better than the Bible for business. The Bible is good for souls. My book is good for wallets.

---

### **The Wives**

**Ivana.** 1977. Beautiful. Manager. We built the empire. But I work too hard. I’m married to the deal.

**Marla.** 1993. Georgia Peach. The wedding was huge. Tiffany came along. Great kid.

Relationships are hard when you're a genius. It’s lonely at the top. But the view is nice.

---

### **The Near-Death Experience**

1990. The crash.

The banks panicked. "Donald, you owe billions."

```text
INTERNAL MEMO: CITIBANK / CHASE / BANKERS TRUST
DATE: August 1990
SUBJECT: THE TRUMP SITUATION

We are in deep trouble. He owes us $3.4 Billion. 
If we bankrupt him, we lose everything.
If we keep him alive, he might pay us back (unlikely).
Conclusion: Put him on an allowance ($450,000/month). 
God help us all.
```

I sat in the room. I looked at them. "If I go down, you go down."

I sold the yacht. I sold the airline.

I survived.

They wrote my obituary. "Trump is Finished."

I cut it out. I framed it.

I wasn't finished. I was just reloading.
