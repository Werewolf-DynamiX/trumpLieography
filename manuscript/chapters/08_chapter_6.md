### **The Moon Feud**

October 2025.

I looked up.

The Moon. Half full.

I called my advisors. "What's going on with the Moon?"

One of them—very smart guy, Princeton—said, "Sir, it's the natural lunar cycle. Gravity and Earth's position create the phases."

I said, "So it's broken."

"No, Sir. It's physics."

"Physics can be better."

I signed an Executive Order: "The Moon shall be full every night. No exceptions."

NASA called. Panicked. "Sir, that's gravitationally impossible—"

"Nothing is impossible. I built a tower taller than God intended. The Moon is nothing."

Cost? Astronomical. But worth it.

China complained. Xi called. "Mr. Trump, you cannot control the Moon."

He hung up. Jealous.

The technology is incredible. Laser-guided mirror drones. Positioned at precise angles.

By 2027, the Moon will be full every single night. Romantic for couples. Better for farmers. Perfect tides.

If the mirrors don't work, we blow it up and build a new moon. We have the technology. Classified.

The haters said, "You can't change the Moon!"
